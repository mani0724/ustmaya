Parenting Masterclass @ 199
We offer Level 1 workshops for parents of children aged
0 - 2 years, 90 minutes
2 - 5 years, 90 minutes
5 - 8 years, 90 minutes

Parenting Masterclass is for Rs 199 and 90 minutes duration. Parents are hand held through an understanding of some of the typical challenges of the life stage, recognising their areas of concerns and offered guidance to make changes. Participants will get a workbook to continue their learning journey. Parents will also get to participate in two group sessions along with fellow participants where their questions and doubts will be individually answered.

Our Other Offerings

Utsmaya offers confidential therapy sessions for:

Children between 8-18 years. Parental consent is necessary
Individual adults and couples

Our therapists are well trained and as ethical practitioners are in personal therapy themselves and in regular supervision. The above two points are mandatory for mental health practitioners to follow ethical standards in the industry.
